Drink Me
Medium: acrylic on 62 water bottles
The word “drink me” in my dialect also means “lie to me”. We will be in a future where voice user interfaces and recommendation algorithms play major roles in our daily life. Will it be a better life?

I drank and collected around 100 water bottles of SmartWater and Soylent. The process of drinking and eating a product became the central focus of this experiment. I used black and white acrylic to smear the brand name and I made it into a video sculpture.
